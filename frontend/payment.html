Game Rules and Regulations

  • The competition lasts one gameweek.
  • Players are randomly assigned to leagues of 10 players.
  • The player with the highest points per group collects the reward.
  • In case of a tie, bench points are considered.
  • Cheats like Bench Boost, Triple Captain, and FPL Manager are allowed to improve your chances of winning.
  • Entry fee: Kshs 50
  • The website retains 10% of the entry fee as its commission.
  • Players must register before the deadline: 2 hours before the first game begins.
